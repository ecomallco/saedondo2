About SAED

We are leading discovery and Innovation since 2012

The Skill Acquisition and Entrepreneurship Development (SAED) was established in march, 2012, with the aim of building the capacity of young graduates as well as to provide the enabling conditions for them to cope with the challenges of unemployment and wealth creation. The NYSC SAED Project was solely conceived in response to the schemes’’ quest to profer solution to post service unemployment and to achieve one of the scheme’s main objectives of promoting self-reliance among Nigerian Youths. The Department is charged with the responsibility of promoting self-reliance and wealth creation through Skill Acquisition and Entrepreneurship Development.

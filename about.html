SAED History

The Skill Acquisition and Entrepreneurship Development (SAED) was established in march , 2012, with the aim of building the capacity of young graduates as well as to provide the enabling conditions for them to cope with the challenges of unemployment and wealth creation.

SAED Mission

To create a platform that will facilitate access of young graduates to requisite skills and resources for successful entrepreneurship as means of enhancing national development

SAED Vision

To develop the culture of self-reliance in our youth (by equipping them with the necessary skills) for sustainable job creation towards national development
